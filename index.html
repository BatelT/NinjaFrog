About NinjaFrog

NinjaFrog is designed to provide a variety of open-source security tools that assist organizations in enhancing their security posture. Similar to Chaos Monkey, NinjaFrog allows you to simulate real-world security challenges and prepare your team to effectively respond to incidents.
