GScan: Google Drive Secret Detector

About GScan

GScan is a tool that performs thorough scans of Google Drive to search for and detect sensitive information, ensuring that your documents and shared files are secure. Therefore, we decided to create the GScan tool that searches and detects secrets in Google Drive. Whether you're an individual or part of a collaborative team, GScan helps you maintain privacy and adhere to compliance regulations by identifying secrets and sensitive data that shouldn't be exposed.
